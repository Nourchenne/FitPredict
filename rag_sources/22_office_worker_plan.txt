TITLE: Office Worker Fat-Loss Strategy

CHALLENGES
Long sitting hours, meetings, low daily movement, stress eating.

DAILY FRAMEWORK
- Protein-focused breakfast.
- Structured lunch (protein + vegetables + quality carbs).
- Planned afternoon snack to avoid vending-machine choices.
- Light dinner with portion control.

MOVEMENT RULES
- 5-minute movement break every 60-90 minutes.
- Walking calls when possible.
- Post-lunch 10-15 minute walk.

TRAINING STRATEGY
- 3 strength sessions/week (30-45 min).
- 2 cardio sessions or step-based cardio equivalent.

STRESS CONTROL
- Keep evening routine: hydration, reduced screen time, fixed bedtime.
