TITLE: Travel and Holiday Weight Management

GOAL
Maintain progress and avoid all-or-nothing behavior while traveling.

PRIORITIES
1. Keep protein at most meals.
2. Stay active (walking, hotel workout, short sessions).
3. Keep hydration high.
4. Maintain sleep as much as possible.

FLEXIBLE RULES
- Use one indulgence at a time.
- Use 80/20 consistency during trips.
- Return to baseline plan immediately after travel.

MINI HOTEL WORKOUT (20-25 min)
- Bodyweight squat
- Push-up variation
- Hip hinge
- Plank
- Walking intervals
