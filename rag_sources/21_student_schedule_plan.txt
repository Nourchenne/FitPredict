TITLE: Student-Friendly Weight Management Plan

CONTEXT
Students often face irregular schedules, exam stress, limited budget, and cafeteria food.

STRATEGY
- Keep a fixed meal skeleton (breakfast, lunch, dinner, optional snack).
- Use quick prep meals on high-workload days.
- Keep protein-rich snacks ready during study sessions.

EXAM PERIOD ADJUSTMENTS
- Reduce workout duration but keep frequency (20-30 min sessions).
- Prioritize sleep consistency to control appetite and stress.
- Avoid replacing meals with only caffeine.

CAMPUS FOOD TIPS
- Build plate around protein + vegetables first.
- Choose water over sugary beverages.
- Use portion awareness for high-calorie items.

MINIMUM TRAINING PLAN
- 3 short full-body sessions/week.
- Daily walking target.
