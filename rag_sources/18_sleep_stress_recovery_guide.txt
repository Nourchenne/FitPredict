TITLE: Sleep, Stress, and Recovery for Fat Loss

WHY IT MATTERS
Poor sleep and high stress can increase hunger, reduce training quality, and impair recovery.

SLEEP BASICS
- Target 7-9 hours/night.
- Keep regular sleep/wake times.
- Reduce screen exposure before bed.
- Limit caffeine late in the day.

STRESS MANAGEMENT
- Short daily breathing or mindfulness (5-10 min).
- Walks after meals.
- Realistic planning to reduce overwhelm.

RECOVERY BASICS
- Alternate hard and easy training days.
- Keep hydration and protein intake consistent.
- Monitor fatigue and adjust volume as needed.

RED FLAGS
- Persistent exhaustion
- Poor motivation for many days
- Declining performance
- Irritability and poor sleep
