TITLE: Budget-Friendly Nutrition for Weight Management

PRINCIPLES
- Buy staple whole foods in bulk.
- Build meals around protein + fiber + simple carbs.
- Cook in batches.

LOW-COST PROTEIN IDEAS
- Eggs
- Canned tuna/sardines
- Chicken thighs
- Lentils/chickpeas/beans
- Greek yogurt

LOW-COST CARB/FIBER IDEAS
- Oats
- Rice
- Potatoes
- Frozen vegetables
- Seasonal fruits

EXAMPLE DAY (LOW COST)
- Breakfast: oats + yogurt + fruit
- Lunch: rice + lentils + vegetables
- Snack: boiled eggs + apple
- Dinner: chicken + potatoes + salad

COST CONTROL TIPS
- Use a fixed grocery list.
- Avoid shopping hungry.
- Replace sugary drinks with water/tea.
- Plan leftovers intentionally.
