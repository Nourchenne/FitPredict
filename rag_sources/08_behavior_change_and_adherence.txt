TITLE: Behavior Change for Sustainable Weight Management

WHY ADHERENCE MATTERS
Long-term consistency is more important than short-term perfection.

HABIT LOOP FRAMEWORK
- Cue: trigger (time/place/emotion)
- Routine: behavior (meal, walk, workout)
- Reward: immediate positive feedback

IMPLEMENTATION INTENTIONS
Use if-then plans:
- If I feel like snacking late, then I drink water and wait 10 minutes.
- If I miss a workout, then I do a 15-minute walk the same day.

MINIMUM EFFECTIVE ACTIONS
- 8,000-10,000 steps/day target (adapt to baseline).
- 2-3 strength sessions/week.
- 80% meals aligned with plan.

MOTIVATION TO SYSTEMS
Do not rely on motivation alone.
Create systems:
- Fixed grocery list
- Fixed meal options
- Fixed training slots

RELAPSE RECOVERY
A setback is data, not failure.
- Identify trigger
- Adjust environment
- Resume next meal/day, no compensation extremes

PROGRESS METRICS
Track weekly:
- Weight trend
- Waist circumference
- Energy level
- Sleep quality
- Training consistency
