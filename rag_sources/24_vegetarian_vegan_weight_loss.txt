TITLE: Vegetarian and Vegan Fat-Loss Guidance

PROTEIN FOCUS
- Include a protein source at each meal:
  legumes, tofu, tempeh, soy yogurt, eggs/dairy (if vegetarian).

MEAL STRUCTURE
- Protein + vegetables + whole-food carbs + healthy fats.
- Keep ultra-processed vegan products occasional.

MICRONUTRIENT AWARENESS
- Iron, B12, calcium, omega-3, and vitamin D may need attention.

PRACTICAL TIPS
- Batch-cook legumes and grains.
- Build high-protein bowls and soups.
- Track protein adequacy daily.
