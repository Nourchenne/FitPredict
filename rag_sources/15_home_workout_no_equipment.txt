TITLE: Home Workout (No Equipment) for Fat Loss

WEEKLY STRUCTURE
- 3 full-body sessions/week
- 2 brisk-walk or cardio days
- 1 active recovery day

SESSION TEMPLATE (30-40 min)
1. Warm-up (5 min): mobility + easy marching
2. Circuit A (3 rounds)
- Squats
- Push-ups (wall/knee/full)
- Glute bridge
- Plank
3. Circuit B (3 rounds)
- Reverse lunges
- Pike press or incline push-up
- Hip hinge (good morning)
- Dead bug
4. Cooldown (5 min)

PROGRESSION
- Increase reps first, then sets, then tempo.
- Keep 1-2 reps in reserve (avoid failure every set).

CARDIO OPTIONS
- Brisk walking
- Stair intervals
- Low-impact dance cardio

MINIMUM TARGET
- 8,000+ steps/day where possible.
