TITLE: Diseases Related to Obesity

1. Type 2 Diabetes
Cause: Insulin resistance
Prevention: Weight loss, exercise

2. Hypertension
High blood pressure due to excess fat tissue.

3. Cardiovascular Disease
Blocked arteries due to cholesterol.

4. Sleep Apnea
Breathing interruption during sleep.

5. Fatty Liver Disease
Fat accumulation in liver cells.

6. Osteoarthritis
Joint damage from excess weight.

7. Metabolic Syndrome
Cluster:
- High blood pressure
- High blood sugar
- Abdominal fat
- Abnormal cholesterol
