TITLE: Types of Diets

1. Mediterranean Diet
Rich in vegetables, olive oil, fish, nuts.
Reduces cardiovascular risk.

2. Low-Carb Diet
Limits carbohydrates.
Helps control insulin.

3. Ketogenic Diet
Very low carb, high fat.
For fat adaptation.

4. High-Protein Diet
Increases satiety.
Supports muscle retention.

5. Intermittent Fasting
Time-restricted eating (e.g., 16:8).

6. Vegetarian Diet
Plant-based with dairy/eggs.

7. Vegan Diet
Fully plant-based.

8. DASH Diet
Designed to reduce hypertension.

CHOOSING A DIET
Depends on:
- Health condition
- Activity level
- Sustainability
- Doctor advice
