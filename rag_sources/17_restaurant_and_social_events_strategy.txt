TITLE: Restaurant and Social Events Strategy

BEFORE EVENT
- Decide your main target: protein + vegetables first.
- Avoid arriving very hungry.

AT THE EVENT
- Start with water.
- Choose grilled/roasted over fried.
- Keep sauces on the side.
- Limit liquid calories.

PRACTICAL RULES
- 80/20 approach: mostly aligned choices, some flexibility.
- One indulgence at a time (dessert or alcohol, not both).

AFTER EVENT
- Return to normal plan at next meal.
- No punishment cardio or extreme restriction.

MINDSET
- One meal does not ruin progress.
- Consistency over weeks matters most.
