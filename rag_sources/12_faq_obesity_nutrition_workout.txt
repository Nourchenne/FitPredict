TITLE: FAQ for Obesity, Nutrition, and Workouts

Q: What is the best diet for obesity?
A: The best diet is the one you can sustain, with a moderate calorie deficit, enough protein, and nutrient-dense foods.

Q: How fast should I lose weight?
A: A steady pace is usually safer and more sustainable than rapid loss.

Q: Is cardio enough?
A: Cardio helps, but combining cardio with strength training improves outcomes and supports muscle retention.

Q: Do I need to remove carbs completely?
A: Not necessarily. Focus on quality and portions rather than total elimination.

Q: How important is sleep?
A: Very important. Poor sleep can worsen hunger regulation and recovery.

Q: What should I track?
A: Weekly weight trend, waist, training consistency, sleep, and energy.

Q: What if progress stalls?
A: Recheck portions, activity, sleep, and adherence. Make small adjustments, not extreme changes.
