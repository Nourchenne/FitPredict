TITLE: Beginner 30-Day Kickstart

WEEK 1
- Build consistency: meals, hydration, walking.
- 2 light full-body sessions.

WEEK 2
- Increase to 3 training sessions.
- Improve meal structure and protein intake.

WEEK 3
- Add one cardio session.
- Tighten snack quality and portions.

WEEK 4
- Maintain routine and review results.
- Plan next month based on adherence and progress.

DAILY CHECKLIST
- Protein at each meal
- Vegetables at least twice/day
- Hydration target
- Movement target
- Sleep routine

SUCCESS CRITERIA
- Better consistency
- Better energy
- Better weekly trend
