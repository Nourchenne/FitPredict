TITLE: Cardio and Strength Protocols for Fat Loss

OBJECTIVE
Improve fat loss, metabolic health, and body composition safely.

WEEKLY BASELINE
- Cardio: 150-300 minutes moderate intensity OR 75-150 minutes vigorous.
- Strength: 2-4 sessions/week covering major muscle groups.

BEGINNER STRENGTH TEMPLATE (FULL BODY)
Session A:
- Squat pattern
- Push pattern
- Row pattern
- Core
Session B:
- Hinge pattern
- Pull pattern
- Press pattern
- Core

GUIDELINES
- 2-4 sets per exercise
- 6-15 reps each set
- Progressive overload over weeks

CARDIO OPTIONS
- Brisk walking
- Cycling
- Swimming
- Elliptical
- Intervals (if tolerated)

INTENSITY CUES
- Moderate: can talk in short sentences.
- Vigorous: difficult to speak full sentences.

RECOVERY RULES
- Keep at least 1 easier day per week.
- Sleep 7-9h.
- Do not increase volume and intensity too fast simultaneously.
