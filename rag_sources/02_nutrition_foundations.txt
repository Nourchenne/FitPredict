TITLE: Nutrition Fundamentals

MACRONUTRIENTS

1. Proteins
Function: muscle repair, hormone production
Sources: chicken, fish, eggs, lentils, tofu, Greek yogurt
Recommended intake: 1.2–2g per kg body weight (active people)

2. Carbohydrates
Function: main energy source
Sources: rice, oats, fruits, vegetables, whole grains
Prefer complex carbs over refined sugars.

3. Fats
Function: hormone regulation, brain health
Healthy fats: olive oil, avocado, nuts, seeds
Avoid trans fats.

MICRONUTRIENTS

Vitamins:
- Vitamin A: vision
- Vitamin C: immunity
- Vitamin D: bone health
- B-complex: metabolism

Minerals:
- Iron
- Calcium
- Magnesium
- Zinc

HYDRATION
- 2–3 liters water per day
- More if exercising

CALORIE BALANCE
- Weight loss = calorie deficit
- Weight gain = calorie surplus
- Maintenance = balance
