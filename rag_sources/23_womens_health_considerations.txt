TITLE: Women-Specific Weight Management Considerations

IMPORTANT
This is educational guidance, not a medical diagnosis.

KEY POINTS
- Weight fluctuations can occur due to cycle phase and water retention.
- Track trends over weeks, not single-day scale changes.
- Protein intake and resistance training are critical for body composition.

PRACTICAL RECOMMENDATIONS
- Keep sleep and stress management as core priorities.
- Use moderate calorie deficits; avoid crash dieting.
- Adjust training intensity based on recovery and energy.

WHEN TO SEEK PROFESSIONAL SUPPORT
- Menstrual irregularity
- Persistent fatigue or dizziness
- History of disordered eating
- Hormonal or endocrine concerns
