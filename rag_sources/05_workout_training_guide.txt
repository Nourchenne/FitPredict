TITLE: Training Guide for Weight Loss

BENEFITS OF EXERCISE
- Burns calories
- Improves metabolism
- Builds muscle
- Reduces stress
- Improves insulin sensitivity

TYPES OF WORKOUTS

1. Cardio
Examples:
- Walking
- Running
- Cycling
- Jump rope
Duration: 30–45 minutes

2. Strength Training
- Squats
- Push-ups
- Lunges
- Deadlifts
Frequency: 3–4 times per week

3. HIIT
Short intense intervals.
Effective for fat loss.

4. Mobility & Flexibility
- Stretching
- Yoga

WEEKLY PLAN EXAMPLE
Monday: Strength
Tuesday: Cardio
Wednesday: Rest
Thursday: HIIT
Friday: Strength
Saturday: Walking
Sunday: Rest
