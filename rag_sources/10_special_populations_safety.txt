TITLE: Safety Considerations and Special Cases

IMPORTANT
This guidance is educational and does not replace professional medical advice.

WHEN TO SEEK MEDICAL SUPERVISION
- BMI in severe obesity range
- Diabetes, hypertension, cardiovascular disease
- Chronic pain or mobility limitations
- Pregnancy/postpartum
- Eating disorder history
- Use of weight-related medications

SAFETY CHECKPOINTS
- Start progressively.
- Monitor blood pressure and symptoms if at risk.
- Stop exercise and seek care for chest pain, severe dizziness, or unusual shortness of breath.

NUTRITION RED FLAGS
- Very low-calorie crashes
- Removing entire food groups without reason
- Severe restriction followed by binge cycles

EXERCISE RED FLAGS
- Pain that worsens with training
- No recovery days
- Sudden volume spikes

PRACTICAL ADAPTATIONS
- Use low-impact cardio for joint issues.
- Increase non-exercise movement (walking, stairs).
- Prioritize form and controlled progression in strength work.
