TITLE: Ramadan and Fasting-Friendly Weight Management

GENERAL APPROACH
- Focus on hydration and nutrient density between fasting windows.
- Avoid overeating at first meal after fasting.

SUHOOR GUIDELINES
- Include protein + fiber + slow carbs.
- Keep sodium moderate to reduce thirst.

IFTAR GUIDELINES
- Break fast gradually.
- Prioritize balanced meal over fried and sugar-heavy options.

TRAINING TIMING
- Light activity before iftar or moderate training after iftar.
- Reduce intensity if recovery is low.

SAFETY
- If dizziness, severe fatigue, or medical conditions are present, seek medical advice.
