TITLE: Weekly Coaching Templates

TEMPLATE A: FAT LOSS PHASE
- Target: moderate calorie deficit
- Protein: each meal
- Training: 3 strength + 2 cardio sessions
- Steps: daily baseline + gradual increase
- Sleep: 7-9h
- Review: weekly trend and adherence

TEMPLATE B: MAINTENANCE PHASE
- Target: calorie balance
- Protein and vegetables remain high
- Training: maintain frequency
- Flex meals: planned, not impulsive
- Review: weight stability and performance

TEMPLATE C: BUSY SCHEDULE
- 30-minute training blocks
- 2 batch-cooked meals + 1 quick meal option
- Walking meetings or short activity breaks
- Weekend prep for weekdays

WEEKLY REVIEW QUESTIONS
1. What worked best this week?
2. What was the main obstacle?
3. What one change will I apply next week?
4. Is my plan realistic for my current schedule?
